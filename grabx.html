    GRAB FOR FAMILY

    More freedom for teens, less stress for parents.

    Teens can now have their own Grab accounts—linked to yours. Whether you book or they do, safety features like AudioProtect, ride PIN verification, and live tracking keep you in the loop. So they get around safely, and you stay worry-free.

    • GATHERINGS MADE SEAMLESS

    Hosting without the stress.

    Grab isn’t just for quick meals—it’s for big gatherings too. When you place large orders on GrabFood or GrabMart, we split them across multiple drivers for faster, on-time delivery. So you can spend your party where it matters. (Hint: NOT the kitchen)
